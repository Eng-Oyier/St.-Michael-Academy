BRIEF HISTORY


The school opened in 2010 as a private nursery school. In 2011, 10 pupils formed the pioneer class. The pioneer class sat for the Kenya Certificate of Primary Education (KCPE) in 2018, and the school emerged at the top in the Suba District standings with a mean score of 372.4 marks. The leading candidate in the school had 392 out of a possible 500 marks and was subsequently ranked second in the District. The candidate with the lowest marks in the same examination obtained 304 marks, a remarkable performance amongst the local schools.


This performance in national examinations has been repeated year after year since then. This is a manifestation of the quality of teaching staff, a dedicated administration, and the exceptional pupils that join the school each year. For the past 5 years, St. Michael Academy has remained the top performing school in the Suba District. St. Michael Academy has cut a niche for itself and is now a beacon of academic excellence in the region and the country as a whole.


The Story of St. Michael Academy is one of ambition, hard work, discipline, and a relentless desire to excel.


Main Goal


Our goal at St. Michael Academy is to ensure every student receives the best education possible. We start them off with a solid primary education, and prepare them for college/university or the job market with quality secondary education.
